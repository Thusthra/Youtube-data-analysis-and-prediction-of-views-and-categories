Key Insights
View Distribution

The view count distribution is typically right-skewed, with most videos having moderate views and a few viral videos with extremely high view counts.

Upload Timing

Videos uploaded during peak hours (evening) and weekdays tend to perform better in terms of initial engagement.

Engagement Correlation

There's a strong positive correlation between likes, comments, and views. Higher engagement typically leads to better algorithmic promotion.

Category Performance

Music and Entertainment categories tend to have the highest average views, while Education and Howto categories show more consistent engagement.

Recommendations for Content Creators
Title Optimization

Keep titles between 50-60 characters. Include relevant keywords and make them compelling to increase click-through rates.

Tag Strategy

Use 5-10 relevant tags. Mix broad and specific tags to maximize discoverability across different search queries.

Description

Write detailed descriptions (200+ characters). Include keywords naturally and add timestamps for longer videos.

Upload Schedule

Upload consistently during peak hours (2-4 PM local time). Maintain a regular posting schedule to build audience expectations.

Engagement

Respond to comments within the first hour. Ask questions in your videos to encourage viewer interaction and boost engagement metrics.

Analytics

Regularly review your analytics to understand what content performs best. Double down on successful formats and topics.
